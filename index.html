Tips & Best Practices

  • Tip 1: Regularly inspect your hives to check for any signs of pests or diseases.
  • Tip 2: Maintain optimal hive temperature by providing adequate ventilation.
  • Tip 3: Ensure your bees have a consistent source of water nearby.
  • Tip 4: Rotate your frames to prevent wax moth infestations and maintain hive hygiene.
  • Tip 5: Keep records of your hive inspections and maintenance activities.
